'Deadpool 2' Has Surpassed 'Logan' At The Domestic Box Office

Date published: May 31, 2018 - 5:02 PM

Just four weeks into its box office run, Deadpool 2 has officially grossed more at the box office than fellow Fox movie Logan. Heading into its fourth weekend, Deadpool 2 has grossed over $231 million domestically while Logan finished its run in the United States with $225 million.

As of this writing, Logan still has the highest gross worldwide with $619 million compared to Deadpool 2's $522.9 million.
