Instructions for the Test

  • There will be 25 questions of a varying difficulty, each worth 1 point. Incorrect answers do not subtract points.
  • The time for the test is 25 minutes from loading the page. Once the timer expires, you will instantly be sent to the correction page.
  • Copying or browsing the internet is strictly forbidden and will be a reason for suspension.
  • When answering a Textfield type question, if the missing word is at the start of the sentence, it has to start with a capital letter, and vice-versa.
  • Checkbox and Multiple Answer type quesions can have from one to four correct answers.
  • Before submitting the exam, please check all your answers.
