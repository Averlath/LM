Hearthstone has two formats: Standard and Wild


Standard format

Standard format is intended to feature a fresher and more focused Hearthstone experience, with a limited pool of cards allowing greater design space, a more dynamically shifting meta, more balanced play, and an easier entry for new players. In order to achieve that, the Standard format limits the playable cards to the ones released in the previous two Hearthstone years, in addition to the Basic and Classic sets.


Standard Years

Standard has had two different rotations so far, they are: Year of the Kraken and Year of the Mammoth.


Wild format

If you're looking for a format in which crazy and wild things happen, you've definitely found it! There are no restrictions in this format, which means every single Hearthstone card is allowed!
